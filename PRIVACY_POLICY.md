# Privacy Policy for Who Owes Me

**Effective Date:** April 2026

Chiradhi ("we", "us", or "our") built the **Who Owes Me** app as a Free application. This SERVICE is provided by us at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

### 1. Data Collection and Local Storage
"Who Owes Me" is explicitly designed as a privacy-focused, offline-first application. 
**All financial data, transaction histories, and notes that you enter into the app are stored exclusively on your device's local database (Room/SQLite).** We do not collect, transmit, intercept, or store your personal or financial data on any external servers or third-party cloud services.

### 2. Required Permissions
The app requests certain permissions strictly for core functionality:
- **Contacts (`READ_CONTACTS`):** Used locally to allow you to easily select names and phone numbers from your address book to link to transactions. Your contact list is never uploaded, tracked, or shared by us.
- **Storage/Media (`WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`):** Used solely to allow you to generate and save PDF statements of your transaction histories directly to your device storage.
- **Biometrics:** If you enable the App Lock feature, the app securely interfaces with your device's native biometric hardware (fingerprint/face unlock). The app does not handle or store raw biometric data.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics trackers, crash reporters, or advertising networks. Your data remains strictly your own.

### 4. Security
We value your trust in tracking your personal financial data. Because the app operates offline, your data's primary security reliance is on the physical security of your device and Android's sandboxed environment. We highly recommend utilizing the built-in Biometric App Lock for added protection.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at **devchiradhi@gmail.com**.
